It was two years back, when I have just passed out from my collage . I didn't get any job from my collage . At first I was not so worried about it . I thought I could manage to get a job soon. But something happened and the whole planet was in a stand still mode, it was covid . It was the time when I started to get tensed , but I thought this will pass. I ignored it and kept on searching for job . But the situation didn't improve, it got worse day by day. As the days went by my thoughts kept growing, I started to get desperate to get a job. I started to lower my expectations day by day . I started to contact friends and relatives to get any job. Though everyone told they will try their best but I got a sense that those are just courtesy. Days passed by and now it has been one and half year I was unemployed. I was broken and didn't know what to do . So I did the easiest thing that I could have done. I started to divert myself , I stated to drink and smoke keep my self away from home, away from family gatherings for as long as I could just to avoid the obvious question from my parents -"what next". The days were passing fast I kept myself busy with friends, enjoying and doing everything to distract myself. But deep down I was unhappy, I was in a so much bad mental state I couldn't sleep at night. My parents gave up hope on me they even stopped asking me after a point about my life. Instead of having so many friends and relatives I felt that I was alone, there was no one who understood my feelings. Finally one day something happened that was an eye opener for me. I was with my friends and the was a friend whom I have met after a long he asked me what I was doing , I told nothing . He replied "then what's the point of so much studying and wasting your parents so much money". I was speechless . I never expected this type of insult. Many of my friends were there expecting me to come up with a come back line but the question shook me. I couldn't say anything . I just smiled back. The incident hurt me , it hurt my Ego. I started thinking how get revenge and get back to him. But as I thought of it I realized, getting back at him is not a solution. I realized that may be he told that on my face but there are many people who think the same thing . I realized how I was wasting my life and have changed to someone who is a shame for his parents. I told myself if I give up and forget this incident then I am looser. I decided to fight for my pride not physically but with my success story. I told myself anyhow I have to start a career. I started to see my past mistakes , how I was too lazy at times, how I have wasted my precious time; and also how I can do a new start. I decided to learn Software Development . It was hard , I knew nothing . I had to learn everything from scratch. But I told myself only one thing - "Six month of struggle sixty years of life". I kept pushing myself . Finally at a stage I started enjoying coding . In quest of my self respect I found my life's path. All the negative thoughts started reducing day by day . Finally now I am in a state where I can forgive myself for all my mistakes and look forward to life and success which I hope to achieve soon. - Tuhin
